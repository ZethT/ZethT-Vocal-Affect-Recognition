Behind the Scenes

How It Works

Each time you upload a file, three steps run automatically on the server.

Step 01

Feature Extraction

librosa and Praat extract 17 acoustic measurements from the audio: pitch (f0), timbral brightness (spectral centroid and rolloff), vocal cleanliness (HNR), and 13 MFCC coefficients capturing tone color and texture.

Step 02

Validation

Each extracted value is checked against expected ranges: pitch must fall between 50 and 500 Hz, and HNR must be above −20 dB. Inputs outside these bounds are flagged as invalid rather than passed to the model.

Step 03

Classification

The 17 features are normalized and fed into the deployed Logistic Regression model, which returns a predicted class label, a confidence score, and the probability for each vocal class.
